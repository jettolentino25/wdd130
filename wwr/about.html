The Great Northern Rush

Our mission is to provide the most exhilarating and safe white water rafting experience in Cagayan de Oro. We believe in adventure that respects nature and empowers the local community.

History

The Cagayan de Oro River has long been part of local life, flowing from the mountains of Bukidnon through the city and into Macajalar Bay. Organized whitewater rafting began in 1995 when members of the Northern Mindanao Mountaineering Society explored the river’s rapids. Over time, rafting evolved into a formal tourism activity with improved safety standards, trained guides, and structured routes, earning Cagayan de Oro recognition as the Whitewater Rafting Capital of the Philippines.
